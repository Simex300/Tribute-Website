Sipnosis


It has been a month since a comet that visits Earth every thousand years is seen from Japan. Mitsuha is a student who laments her life in the country with her little sister, grandmother and father, a politician she never sees. He detests the picturesque customs of his family and dreams of the wonderful lifestyle of the inhabitants of Tokyo. For his part, Taki is a student living in Tokyo, has a part-time job in an Italian restaurant and needs to move away from his family. One night, Mitsuha dreams that he is a boy from Tokyo and Taki dreams that she is a girl who lives in the countryside. What is the secret that is hidden behind the dreams of two people who, although they do not know anything, seem desperately seeking?

Characters


Mitsuha Miyamizu

Mitsuha Miyamizu (宮水 三葉 Miyamizu Mitsuha?) is a 17-year old teenage girl living in a small town called Itomori. She is a part of her family's shrine and participates in old traditions as a shrine maiden with her younger sister Yotsuha. The dislike of her hometown, the issues with her family shrine and the issue with her own father makes Mitsuha want to live as a handsome boy in Tokyo. Her wish would eventually be somewhat granted, as she would start switching bodies with Taki Tachibana. She is the female protagonist of the movie.

Taki Tachibana

Taki Tachibana (立花 瀧 Tachibana Taki?) is a high school boy who lives in the city side of Tokyo. In his daily life, he spends fun times with friends and works part time at an Italian restaurant. He held a secret affection toward his co-worker Miki Okudera and switched bodies with a teenage girl named Mitsuha Miyamizu who lived in a small rural town, in contrast to his lively life is Tokyo. He is the male protagonist of the film..

About Author


Taki Tachibana

Makoto Shinkai (新海 誠 Shinkai Makoto), born as Makoto Niitsu (新津 誠 Niitsu Makoto, born February 9, 1973), is a Japanese director, writer, producer, animator, editor, cinematographer, voice actor, manga artist and former graphic designer.

A native of Nagano Prefecture, Shinkai studied Japanese literature at Chuo University where he was a member of juvenile literature club where he drew picture books. He traces his passion for creation to the manga, anime, and novels he was exposed to while in middle school. His favorite anime is Castle in the Sky by Hayao Miyazaki. Shinkai has been called "The New Miyazaki" in several reviews, including those by Anime Advocates and ActiveAnime—comparisons which he himself calls an "overestimation".
